My philosophy

Photography is more than pressing a button. It's about patience, presence, and the ability to see beauty in the mundane. Every person has a story written in the lines of their face. Every landscape holds a secret in its shadows.

I believe in authentic moments over posed perfection. In natural light over artificial. In connection over direction. My role isn't to create‚Äîit's to witness and preserve.
