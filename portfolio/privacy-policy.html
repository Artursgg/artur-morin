This Privacy Policy explains how Artur Morin ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you visit arturmorin.com.

1. Who We Are

Artur Morin
Visual Storyteller & Photographer
Based in Tallinn, Estonia

Email: hello@arturmorin.com

2. Information We Collect

We only collect personal information that you voluntarily provide through our contact form. This includes:

  • • Full Name — Required for responding to your inquiry
  • • Email Address — Required for responding to your inquiry
  • • Message/Project Details — Optional information you provide about your project

We do not collect any additional personal data unless you submit it yourself.

3. Analytics & Cookies

Our website uses the following services to understand how visitors interact with the site:

Google Analytics

Google Analytics may collect:

  • • IP addresses (anonymized)
  • • Device and browser information
  • • Pages visited and time spent on the site
  • • General location (city/country)

This data is anonymized where possible and used strictly for website performance and user experience improvements. We do not use Google Analytics for advertising or remarketing.

Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Google Tag Manager

We use Google Tag Manager to manage website tags and tracking scripts. This service helps us organize and deploy analytics and marketing tags efficiently.

reCAPTCHA v3

We use Google reCAPTCHA v3 to protect our contact forms from spam and abuse. reCAPTCHA may collect:

  • • Browser and device information
  • • Mouse movements and interactions
  • • IP address

This data is processed by Google to determine if you are a human user. Google's Privacy Policy applies: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you submit in our contact form solely for:

  • • Responding to your inquiries
  • • Communicating with you based on your request
  • • Providing photography services if you become a client

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

We process your personal data based on:

  • • Your consent — when you submit the contact form
  • • Legitimate interest — for basic website analytics and security
  • • Contractual necessity — if you become a client, to fulfill our service agreement

6. Data Retention

We retain contact form submissions only as long as necessary to:

  • • Respond to your request
  • • Maintain business records required by law
  • • Fulfill ongoing client relationships

Analytics data is stored according to Google's own retention policies (typically 14-26 months).

7. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. This includes:

  • • SSL encryption for data transmission
  • • reCAPTCHA protection on forms
  • • Secure email communication

However, no online transmission or storage system is completely secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Your Rights (Under GDPR)

As a data subject under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • • Access your personal data
  • • Rectification — request correction of inaccurate data
  • • Erasure — request deletion of your data
  • • Restrict processing — limit how we use your data
  • • Data portability — receive your data in a structured format
  • • Object to processing of your data
  • • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at hello@arturmorin.com

9. Third-Party Services

The following third-party services process data on this site:

  • • Google Analytics — Website analytics (Privacy Policy)
  • • Google Tag Manager — Tag management (Privacy Policy)
  • • Google reCAPTCHA — Spam protection (Privacy Policy)

These services have their own privacy policies and terms of service.

10. Cookies

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies for:

  • • Analytics (Google Analytics)
  • • Security (reCAPTCHA)
  • • Website functionality

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may affect website functionality.

11. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside the European Economic Area (EEA), including the United States, where Google's servers are located. These transfers are protected by appropriate safeguards, including Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the European Commission.

12. Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

14. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, your data, or wish to exercise your rights, please contact us at:

Email: hello@arturmorin.com

Location: Tallinn, Estonia
